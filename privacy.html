Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Business Manager Pro (“we”, “us”, “our”) helps you generate and share professional e-receipts. We collect and process only what’s needed to operate the service, and you remain the owner of your data.

What we collect

  • Account: email address and authentication identifiers.
  • Business profile: business name, BRN, VAT (if any), address, phone.
  • Clients: company name, BRN, contact name, email/phone, address.
  • Receipts: line items, amounts, currency, taxes, status, reference numbers.
  • Product usage: basic event logs (e.g., sign-in, create receipt) for reliability and abuse prevention.

What we don’t collect

We do not store bank card numbers or online banking credentials. If you pay us via JUICE or PayPal, payment details are processed by those providers.

Why we use your data

  • To provide the app: authentication, saving profiles/clients/receipts, generating PDFs.
  • To secure and improve the service: detect fraud/abuse, fix bugs, plan features.
  • To communicate: account notices, support responses, critical product updates.

Where and how data is stored

We use Supabase (managed Postgres) for storage. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We enforce Row-Level Security (RLS) so your account can only access your own data. Files you upload (e.g., logo) are stored in Supabase Storage.

Sharing

We don’t sell personal data. Limited access by staff may occur for troubleshooting or support and is subject to confidentiality. When you share a receipt via link, anyone with that link can view the receipt—treat links as public unless protected.

Retention & deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You can request export (CSV/PDF) or deletion at any time. Backups may persist for a short period after deletion for disaster recovery.

Your rights

You may access, correct, export, or delete your data. Contact us and we’ll respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Children

The service is not intended for children under 16. Do not sign up or submit data for minors.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be highlighted on this page.
